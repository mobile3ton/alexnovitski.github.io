About

I draw the picture that makes the idea click.

I'm an illustrator working across editorial, character, and brand illustration. Most projects start with a messy brief and a deadline — my job is to find the one image that says what the words can't.

Based in Warsaw, working with clients everywhere. Past work has run in print magazines, on packaging, and inside a couple of games nobody has heard of yet.

Open for freelance commissions and longer-term collaborations. Reach out at hello@adareyes.art.
