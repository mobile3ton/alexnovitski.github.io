The Star

Year — 2025 Medium — Gouache, finished digitally Client — Harbor Quarterly Role — Illustrator

A six-page spread for Harbor Quarterly's food issue, following three vendors through one night at the city's oldest night market. The brief asked for something that felt "loud on a quiet page" — so the palette leans into the market's actual lighting: sodium-orange stalls against a near-black sky.
